Nature Destination

Explore the Natural Beauty of East Java

Enjoy an adventure amidst the natural wonders of East Java, from majestic volcanoes to stunning white sandy beaches. Discover the mesmerizing beauty of nature and experience unforgettable peace amidst breathtaking natural panoramas.

Explore

Most Popular Destination

Mount Bromo

An unforgettable tourist icon of East Java, offering majestic natural panoramas and an immersive adventure experience. With majestic craters and vast deserts, enjoy the unique thrill of a jeep ride or watch the sunrise at Bukit Cinta for unforgettable moments.

Ijen Crater

Kawah Ijen is a stunning natural destination with its rare blue fire phenomenon and exotic scenery. When night falls, the dazzling Blue Fire emanates from the bottom of the crater, adding to its magical appeal.

Tumpak Sewu Waterfall

Tumpak Sewu Waterfall amazes with its spectacular natural beauty. The clear water that falls from a height of about 120 meters, surrounded by lush green tropical vegetation, creates an enchanting and calming atmosphere for visitors.

Best Activities For You to Experience

Enjoy enchanting natural wonders and immersive adventures amidst breathtaking natural beauty.

Mountain Climbing
Mountain climbing activities are an unforgettable experience in natural tourist destinations such as Mount Bromo, Mount Semeru and Kawah Ijen. Climbing mountains not only provides a physical challenge, but also offers stunning natural views. From the top of the mountain, visitors can enjoy a wide panorama, especially at sunrise or sunset. These mountains are also often a destination for photographers to capture spectacular natural beauty.
